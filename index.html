About Kaaj Cafe

Kaaj Café was founded by alumni to give back to the organization that shaped our educational journeys. More than a café, it’s a space of hope, friendship, and new beginnings.

Every cup and pastry supports the Girls to Uni Program, helping young women pursue higher education and become future leaders. Built on gratitude and the belief in community, Kaaj Café turns every visit into a step toward empowering the next generation.
